How we met

Yana and Dennis met in the Fall of 2016 during an introductory Biology class at George Washington University. When Yana (rather unfairly) gave Dennis a 5/100 on his first quiz, Dennis dropped the class. Their paths wouldn't cross again until next Fall. Dennis took Yana on a coffee date, and they hit it off. Their first dinner date was at El Centro in Georgetown. Yana told Dennis she's aspiring to go to medical school. Dennis was playing D1 tennis and working towards his Computer Science degree. Yana's brilliancy and hard work led her to the University of Miami Miller School of Medicine in 2020. Meanwhile, Dennis got his first job in Boulder, Colorado. After a year of long distance, they moved in together in 2021. Dennis and Yana got engaged in Sarasota on December 6th, 2023. Since then, Yana began her career as an anesthesiologist while Dennis worked at NASA Goddard for a period of time before moving to Oracle.
